Recombinant Human Anti-Ang2 x Human Anti-VEGFA Bispecific Antibody (Tandem Diabody)  (CAT#: T-BSDB-H194)

Recombinant Anti-Ang2 x Anti-VEGFA Bispecific Antibody (Tandem Diabody) is designed to be expressed as a monomeric subunit (single chain Diabody, scDb), with four variable domains from two parental antibodies. Then the two peptide chains can dimerize in a head-to-tail fashion. This BsAb is capable of inhibiting two proangiogenics fators and a combined blockade of them has a greater inhibitory efficacy compared with inhibition of either factor alone. It is designed for the research of Colorectal cancer (CRC); Solid tumors; Wet AMD (Age-related macular degeneration); Diabetic macular edema therapy.
